The Cake Factory

The Cake Factory is a game where you can build your own cake by choosing the flavor, the number of layers, whether to have a candle or not, the decorations, and the writing on the cake.


Obstacle Course

Obstacle Course is a game where you can pick a theme, between space, desert, and underwater, then use the arrow keys to move your gamepiece to the finish line without hitting any of the obstacles. If you reach the finish line in the given time, you win, and you get to be on the leaderboard.


Halloween Candy Catcher

Halloween Candy Catcher is a game where you move the basket left and right to catch candies falling from the sky. Each candy gives you a different number of points. The points you get add up to your final score when the timer is up.
